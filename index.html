Our hotel is a destination for both business and leisure.

The modern design and spacious rooms with private balconies, free Wi-Fi, a variety of gastronomic options, 4 meeting rooms, and 24-hour in-room dining service ensure complete comfort and convenience for the business traveller. The swimming pool and spa allow for relaxation after a long day.

Families will enjoy our Aqua Park and the beach, the very special family rooms and the many kids’ activities and games.
